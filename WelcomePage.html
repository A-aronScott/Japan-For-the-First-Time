Welcome to Japan

Despite it's small size Japan is a country that has countless places to visit and things to do. So much so that planning a first trip can be a bit overwheling. This website is meant to be a guide for first time visitors, a place that offers a few simple suggestions to make your first visit to this beautiful country one to remember.
