General Rules

Japan is a country with a rich culture and history, and it is important to be respectful of local customs and traditions when visiting. Here are some general rules to follow when visiting Japan:

  • Remove your shoes when entering someone's home or traditional establishments.
  • Always be on time
  • Use both hands when giving or receiving something such as a gift.
  • Do not talk loudly in public places, especially on public transportation.
  • Do not eat or drink while walking in public places.
  • Do not tip at restaurants or anywhere else, good service is an expectation not something to be paid for
